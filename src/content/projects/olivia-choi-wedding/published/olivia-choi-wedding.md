# Olivia Choi Photography: AI-Powered Ultra-Fast Web Build for a Chicago Wedding Photographer

Establishing an online presence in Chicago's local business ecosystem is crucial. This project is about building a system that automatically converts on-site stories into marketing assets while elevating the brand value of premium wedding photographer **Olivia Choi**, who operates in downtown Chicago, Lincoln Park, West Loop, and beyond.

---

## 1. Plan (Strategy & 'Why')

### [Promise] High-Performance Portfolio Completed in 3 Days
Without compromising Olivia's high-resolution images, we build a Google search-optimized portfolio website in just 3 days. We also provide an engine that automatically produces blog content solely from shooting records without complex writing after every shoot.

### [Problem] Beautiful Photos Sleeping in Local Folders
During the busy season, weekly shooting schedules left no time for site updates. Building a site through large agencies was too expensive ($5,000+), and template builders were too slow, putting them at a disadvantage for Chicago local search exposure.

### [Solution] Astro.build Ultra-Fast Framework + AI Marketing Pipeline
We build targeting a Google Core Web Vitals score of 100 with Astro architecture that minimizes JS loading. Furthermore, when Olivia inputs simple field notes (shooting location, concept, atmosphere), the system instantly converts them into local SEO-optimized blog posts using AI.
